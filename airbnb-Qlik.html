Maximizing the Airbnb Experience

Project Overview:
This project involves a comprehensive study of Airbnb data from Antwerp, Brussels, and Ghent. The goal is to develop a Business Intelligence dashboard that deciphers complexities in the Airbnb marketplace and presents them intuitively. The project is titled "Maximizing the Airbnb Experience: A Deep Dive into Guest Preferences and Hosting Best Practices."

Target Audience:
The primary audience for this dashboard is Airbnb hosts and potential investors. The dashboard provides insights into guest preferences, market trends, and effective property positioning, helping hosts make informed decisions on pricing, property offerings, and more.

Visualized Information:
The dashboard answers key questions, such as property types with the highest review scores, the relationship between listing features and guest satisfaction, and peak booking seasons. It also identifies top-rated neighborhoods and patterns in pricing and occupancy rates.

Narrative:
The project tells the story of optimizing the Airbnb experience by understanding what makes certain listings more successful. It explores guest preferences and provides hosts with actionable strategies to improve their offerings.

Tools and Technologies:
The dashboard is built using Qlik Sense, Qlik embedding, and Qlik scripting to ensure efficient data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL). The intuitive interface guides users through the data, offering actionable insights and addressing real-world challenges faced by Airbnb hosts.
