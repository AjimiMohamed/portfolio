Rail Safety and Automation with RideOnTrack

Introduction:
This project, in partnership with RideOnTrack, aimed to enhance rail safety and automation through advanced AI-driven solutions. The project utilized U-Net and an autoencoder for detailed image segmentation and YOLO for efficient sign detection, focusing on identifying anomalies along the tracks and accurately detecting train traffic signs.

Project Goals:
The primary goals of this initiative were to improve track safety and streamline the development of railway infrastructure. By leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies, the project sought to provide real-time detection and analysis of track conditions and traffic signs.

Technologies Used:

  • U-Net and Autoencoder: Employed for detailed image segmentation to identify anomalies along the railway tracks.
  • YOLO (You Only Look Once): Used for efficient and accurate detection of train traffic signs.

Key Features:

  • Real-time anomaly detection along railway tracks to enhance safety.
  • Accurate identification of train traffic signs using YOLO.
  • Integration of U-Net and autoencoder models for precise image segmentation.
  • Improvement of railway infrastructure development through advanced AI analytics.

Conclusion:
This project highlights the potential of AI in transforming rail safety and automation. By partnering with RideOnTrack, we successfully developed a system that not only improves the safety of railway operations but also contributes to the efficient development of railway infrastructure. The use of U-Net, autoencoders, and YOLO showcases the capabilities of AI in handling complex tasks in real-time, paving the way for more innovative solutions in the transportation sector.
