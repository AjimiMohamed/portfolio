SharePoint Solution for Client Administration

Introduction:
This project focuses on developing a comprehensive SharePoint solution for a client administration department. The solution includes multiple Power Apps applications and Power Automate workflows to streamline invoice generation, receipt processing, and expense management.

Invoice Generation:
A Power Apps application was developed to generate invoices through SharePoint. Users can click a button that opens the Power Apps app, allowing them to create an invoice. Once created, the invoice is automatically sent to the concerned party using Power Automate.

Incoming Invoice Processing:
Another system listens to the company's inbox for incoming invoices and uploads them to SharePoint. This ensures all incoming invoices are centrally stored and accessible for further processing and record-keeping.

Receipt and Expense Management:
An additional Power Apps application enables employees to upload receipts and expenses. The application uses AI to extract necessary information from the uploaded documents and saves them for review by the employers. This automation reduces manual data entry and increases efficiency in expense management.

Key Features:

  • Automated invoice generation and dispatch using Power Apps and Power Automate.
  • Centralized storage of incoming invoices through automated email monitoring and SharePoint integration.
  • AI-powered receipt and expense management to extract and save relevant information for employer review.
  • User-friendly interface for employees to manage invoices, receipts, and expenses.

Conclusion:
This SharePoint solution demonstrates the effective use of Power Apps, Power Automate, and AI to enhance the efficiency of a client administration department. By automating invoice generation, processing incoming invoices, and managing receipts and expenses, the solution provides a streamlined and efficient workflow for administrative tasks.
